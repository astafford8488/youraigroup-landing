## FRONT OFFICE LEAKS

### Leak #1: Missed calls during jobs
**Estimated cost: $2,000–$5,000/month**

You're on a roof, under a sink, or mid-appointment. The phone rings. You can't answer. That caller tries the next company on Google and books with them instead.

The average service call is worth $300–$800. If you miss just 5–10 calls per week, that's $6,000–$32,000/month walking out the door.

**The AI fix:** A voice AI agent answers every call in under 3 seconds, 24/7. It qualifies the lead, captures job details, and books the appointment — even at 2 AM on a Saturday. You wake up to a full calendar instead of a full voicemail box.

---

### Leak #2: Slow follow-up on leads
**Estimated cost: $1,500–$3,000/month**

A lead fills out your contact form or leaves a voicemail. You get back to them 3 hours later — or tomorrow. By then, they've already booked with someone who responded in 5 minutes.

Research shows that responding within 5 minutes makes you **21x more likely** to convert that lead. After 30 minutes, your odds crater.

**The AI fix:** Automated instant text-back and email response the moment a lead comes in. "Hey, we got your message — when works best for an estimate?" goes out in under 10 seconds, keeping the lead warm until you're free.

---

### Leak #3: No after-hours capture
**Estimated cost: $1,000–$2,500/month**

Your business hours are 8–5, but 40% of service searches happen between 6 PM and 8 AM. Every after-hours call that hits voicemail is a potential customer you'll never hear from.

**The AI fix:** AI voice and chat agents that work nights, weekends, and holidays. They don't just take messages — they book appointments, answer FAQs, and send confirmation texts. Your business runs 24/7 without you lifting a finger.

---

### Leak #4: Website visitors who leave without converting
**Estimated cost: $500–$2,000/month**

Your website gets traffic, but 95%+ of visitors leave without calling, texting, or booking. You're paying for those clicks (SEO or ads), then watching them bounce.

**The AI fix:** An AI chat agent on your website that engages visitors in real-time conversation, answers questions, and captures contact info before they leave. It's like having your best salesperson on the site 24/7.

---

### Leak #5: Leads that go cold
**Estimated cost: $1,000–$3,000/month**

Someone requests a quote but doesn't book. You send one follow-up text (maybe), then forget about them. Meanwhile, they're still shopping — and the company that follows up 5–7 times wins.

**The AI fix:** Automated SMS and email nurture sequences that follow up at the right intervals with the right messages. Day 1: "Still need that estimate?" Day 3: "We had a cancellation — want this slot?" Day 7: "Here's what other [your city] homeowners are saying about us." Hands-free, persistent, not pushy.

---

### Leak #6: Reputation bleeding
**Estimated cost: $500–$2,000/month (indirect)**

Happy customers leave silently. Unhappy ones leave Google reviews. Without a system asking every customer for a review, your star rating slowly drops — and every 0.1-star decrease costs you roughly 5% of your clicks.

**The AI fix:** Automated review requests go out after every completed job via text. Happy customer? Straight to Google. Unhappy? Routed to a private feedback form so you can fix it before it goes public. Your reviews stack up on autopilot.

---

## BACK OFFICE LEAKS

### Leak #7: Manual invoicing delays
**Estimated cost: $500–$1,500/month**

Job's done, but the invoice doesn't go out for 3 days because you're busy with the next job. Then the customer takes 2 weeks to pay because there's no reminder system. Cash flow suffers.

**The AI fix:** Invoice auto-generates the moment a job is marked complete. Payment reminders go out automatically at day 3, 7, and 14. Online payment links mean customers pay from their phone in 30 seconds.

---

### Leak #8: Scheduling chaos
**Estimated cost: $300–$1,000/month**

Double-bookings. Missed appointments. Inefficient routes that burn gas and time. Texts, calls, DMs, and emails all coming in from different places with no central system.

**The AI fix:** One booking calendar that syncs across your website, Google Business Profile, and all lead sources. Automatic confirmations, reminders, and no-show follow-ups. Route optimization if you're dispatch-based.

---

### Leak #9: No-shows eating your schedule
**Estimated cost: $500–$2,000/month**

10–20% of appointments no-show in most service businesses. That's blocked time you can't fill, prep work wasted, and a truck that rolled for nothing.

**The AI fix:** Automated confirmation sequence — text at booking, reminder 24 hours before, reminder 2 hours before. If they don't confirm, the slot opens for someone else. No-show rates drop to under 5%.

---

### Leak #10: Lost repeat business
**Estimated cost: $500–$1,500/month**

A customer used you once, had a great experience, and then... never heard from you again. They forgot your name by the time they need you next and search Google again from scratch.

**The AI fix:** Automated re-engagement campaigns. "Hey [name], it's been 6 months since your last service — want to schedule your next one?" Seasonal reminders, birthday discounts, referral requests. Your past customers become repeat customers.

---

### Leak #11: Wasted time on admin
**Estimated cost: $1,000–$2,000/month**

You or your office manager spend 15+ hours per week on scheduling, reminders, follow-ups, invoicing, and data entry. That's a part-time employee's worth of time — time you could spend on billable work or growing the business.

**The AI fix:** Workflow automation connects your CRM, calendar, invoicing, and communication tools. When a lead comes in → it's auto-assigned → confirmation sent → reminder scheduled → invoice generated → review requested. Zero manual steps.

---

### Leak #12: Flying blind on numbers
**Estimated cost: Hard to quantify — but it's the most expensive leak**

You don't know your close rate, your average job value, your cost per lead, or which marketing channel actually drives revenue. You're making $50K+ decisions on gut feeling.

**The AI fix:** A unified CRM dashboard that shows you the full picture: where leads come from, how many convert, average job value, revenue by source, and ROI on every marketing dollar. Data-driven decisions, not guesswork.
