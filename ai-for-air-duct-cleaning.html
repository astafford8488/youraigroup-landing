What's costing Air Duct Cleaning Companies money right now

First-Call Advantage Goes to Competitors

Duct cleaning customers typically call 2–3 companies and book the first one that answers with a clear price and availability. Missing a call doesn't mean they'll call back — it means they already booked someone else. Each lost call is a $300–$500 whole-home cleaning gone.

Quoting Takes Too Long on the Phone

Every caller wants a price before they commit. Manually asking about home size, vent count, system type, and add-ons like dryer vent cleaning or sanitization turns a quick call into a 10-minute conversation. Multiply that across 20 daily calls and your office is buried in quoting instead of booking.

No Re-Cleaning Reminders Sent

NADCA recommends duct cleaning every 3–5 years, but homeowners never remember. Without automated reminders, you're spending marketing dollars to re-acquire customers you already served. Each lapsed customer is a $300–$500 cleaning you could have booked with a simple text message.

FAQ

Common questions from Air Duct Cleaning Companies

Can AI work for my air duct cleaning business?
Yes. Air duct cleaning is a high-volume, appointment-driven business where speed to answer determines who gets booked. AI answers every call instantly, asks about the home size, number of vents, whether dryer vent cleaning is also needed, and any allergy or air quality concerns. It books the appointment and sends a confirmation text — all before the customer has time to call a competitor.
What does AI automate for air duct cleaning companies?
AI handles 24/7 call answering, ductwork inspection scheduling, dryer vent cleaning add-on upsells, allergen removal service bookings, and post-service review requests. It captures home square footage, vent count, and HVAC system type on every inquiry. It also sends 3–5 year re-cleaning reminders per NADCA guidelines and manages seasonal allergy-season marketing campaigns automatically.
What's the ROI of AI for an air duct cleaning company?
Whole-home duct cleaning averages $300–$500, and dryer vent cleaning add-ons run $100–$200 per visit. Air duct cleaning leads are often one-call-close — the customer books whoever answers first. Recovering just five missed calls per week at $400 average adds $8,000 in monthly revenue. AI also increases dryer vent upsell rates by consistently offering the add-on on every call.
